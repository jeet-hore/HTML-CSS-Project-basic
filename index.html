Events and Shows

Join us for an exciting series of guest speaker sessions hosted by the Computer Science and Engineering department ofSupreme Knowledge Foundation. Our lineup features distinguished speakers from various domains within the tech industry, each bringing a wealth of knowledge and experience. Mr. Manik Kharait, a renowned AI researcher from MIT, will kick off the series with a talk on the future of artificial intelligence and its ethical implications. Following him, Mr. Siddheswar Mondal, CTO of a leading cybersecurity firm, will delve into the latest trends in cybersecurity and how to protect against emerging threats. Mr. Jeet Hore, a senior software engineer at Google, will share his journey in the tech industry and provide valuable career advice for aspiring developers. Lastly, Mr. Rajesh Mondal, a blockchain expert, will explore the potential of blockchain technology beyond cryptocurrencies. These sessions are a unique opportunity to gain insights from top professionals, ask questions, and network with peers. Don't miss out on this chance to expand your knowledge and stay ahead in the ever-evolving field of computer science and engineering.
